Boiled Eggs

Description

Boiled eggs...the most basic of dishes, asides from cracking open those ramen noodles you have in the cupboard. Nothing like the taste of college. Fun fact, hens regularly give eggs that are sterile, which both the farmer and hens know will not yield an offspring. The farmers take these from the coop and they eventually end up in your pot.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Water

Steps

  1. Place eggs in a pot
  2. Pour enough water over the eggs to cover
  3. Cover the pot and wait for the water to boil
  4. Remove pot from the heat, and let it sit covered for 15 minutes
  5. Fill in a large bowl with cold water, and transfer eggs into said bowl
  6. Chill eggs in refrigerator for at least 2 hours before peeling
  7. Enjoy!
